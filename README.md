📊 Telco Customer Churn Analysis

📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer churn behavior in a telecom company using data analysis and visualization techniques. The goal is to identify key factors that influence customer churn and provide actionable insights to improve customer retention.

🎯 Objectives

* Understand the overall churn rate and customer retention
* Identify key factors affecting customer churn
* Analyze customer behavior based on services, tenure, and pricing
* Provide business recommendations to reduce churn


📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains information about telecom customers, including:

* Customer demographics (gender, senior citizen, etc.)
* Account details (tenure, contract type, monthly charges)
* Services subscribed (internet, tech support, security, etc.)
* Payment methods
* Churn status (Yes/No)


🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Google CoLab



📊 Key Analysis Performed

* Data cleaning and preprocessing
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* Visualization of churn patterns using charts
* Comparative analysis across different customer segments



🔍 Key Insights

🔢 Overall Churn
* Around 1 out of 4 customers tend to leave the service
* Indicates a need for strong retention strategies

👥 Demographics
* Gender does not significantly impact churn
* Senior citizens show higher churn due to possible service or pricing challenges

⏳ Tenure
* New customers are more likely to churn
* Long-term customers are more stable and loyal

📃 Contract Type
* Month-to-month contracts show higher churn
* Long-term contracts improve customer retention

🌐 Services
* Fiber optic users show higher churn
* Customers with additional services are more engaged and less likely to leave

💳 Payment Method
* Customers using electronic checks show higher churn
* Auto-pay users are more stable

💰 Pricing
* Higher monthly charges are associated with higher churn
* Customers expect value for the price they pay


📈 Conclusion

Customer churn is mainly influenced by service usage, pricing, and customer experience rather than basic demographics. Improving onboarding, promoting long-term contracts, and enhancing service quality can significantly reduce churn.

🚀 Recommendations
* Improve early customer engagement
* Encourage long-term contracts
* Promote value-added services
* Enhance service quality
* Simplify payment methods
